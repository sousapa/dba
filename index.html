A bit about me

As a Senior Oracle Database Administrator at Alpega, I manage, optimize, and secure Oracle and PostgreSQL environments across multiple clients and projects. With over 20 years of experience in database administration, development, and maintenance, I bring a strong balance of technical depth and practical problem-solving.

I hold Oracle Certified Associate (OCA) and Oracle Certified Professional (OCP) credentials, along with an MBA in Project Management. My background allows me to align technical solutions with business goals while maintaining high standards of reliability and performance.

Recently, I've been focusing on AI agents, acting as an AI champion within the company, exploring practical use cases and integrating intelligent automation into existing workflows.

I'm known for my analytical mindset, troubleshooting skills, and ability to handle complex situations with efficiency and professionalism.
